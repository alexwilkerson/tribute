Richard D. James //Aphex Twin

Richard David James (aka Aphex Twin) is a wild red-headed electronic Grammy Award-winning maniac born August 18th, 1971 in Limerick, Ireland. Some have called him the Mozart of our time. Some have called him the ginger Jesus of analog synthesis. But whatever the epithet ascribed to the dandy musician, it is without dispute that he wields the singular talent of a thousand Grecian polymaths. He has written countless hours of electronic music that is challenging, confounding, and awe-inspiring. He has brought down house upon house—rave upon rave. He single-handedly revolutionized music in the 1990s and the reverberations of his influence can be heard from suburban garages to Kanye West.

"I want your soul!" —Aphex Twin
